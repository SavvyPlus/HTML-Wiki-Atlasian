Admin Tasks : ESCs and VEETs

Created by Kate Daley on Dec 14, 2016

Update the ESCs and VEETs 2-3 times a week.


Go to this link:

http://www.demandmanager.com.au/certificate-prices/

Hover the mouse over the blue and red lines in the chart to show the latest ESC and VEET price and date.

Update the database at: 

SQL (MarketData -> Enviro_ForwardCurve)

Eg. 

ID          Date     Certificate         Vintage Midpoint Price Bid Price            Offer Price        Source  Chart

9594     2016-12-09        ESC       SPOT     20.05    NULL     NULL     Demand Manager          NULL


Update Forward and Spot Prices

Open the spreadsheet:

S:\MarketData\LGCs & STCs\ESC & VEEC Spot and Forward Prices

Open the same link:

http://www.demandmanager.com.au/certificate-prices/

Insert the most recent price from Latest Forward Trades and Latest Spot Trades (tables), and insert into the spreadsheet.
