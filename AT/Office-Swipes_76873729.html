Programming Fobs

  1. On the wall unit next to the fridge enter the code 1985E
  2. Wait until the green light comes on then press *(UserNo)E where the UserNo is the nth programmed fob number (e.g. UserNo 009 is the 9th fob programmed, next fob programmed will be 010 and so on)
    NOTE: refer to the fob access spreadsheet for the list of users and fob numbers: S:\SavvyPlus\Office Swipes.xlsx
  3. Swipe the fob on the card reader on the front door
  4. Return to the wall unit and press E to finalise the programming
  5. Press E on the wall unit again to exit program mode
  6. Update the user number and fob number in the above spreadsheet
