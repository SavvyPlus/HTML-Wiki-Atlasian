WPT Trading Operations : Reconciliation

Created by James Cheong on Feb 23, 2016

Once the invoices have been finalized and the due date arrives, it is necessary to confirm that the correct amounts have been received.
This can be done by logging into the appropriate banking system (currently CBA Commbiz) and checking that the correct amounts have been received.
If they haven't been received, this will need to be chased up.
