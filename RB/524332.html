C14G*

Non-Residential (TOU)All non-residential customers taking supply at less than 1kV

This tariff applies a daily supply charge ($/cust pa) and uses an energy based charge (c/kWh), which varies according to seasonal and time of day parameters. Network energy is computed from a Summer and Non-Summer rate, and broken into Peak, Shoulder and Offpeak. Info needed on suitability

CDS Dedicated Circuit*


*There is a bulk tariff available for each of these and it is denoted by a 'B' on the end of the tariff code. Bulk tariffs are available for customers who are supplied directly from on-site substation terminals where there are no CitiPower distribution assets beyond the substation. Those tariffs with fixed energy rates see a discount in per annum Standing Charges. Whilst tariffs on a flexible pricing schedule or time of use (TOU) see a discount in Peak, Offpeak and Shoulder rates.
