Retail Billing : 2017 Changes- Citipower

Created by Kate Daley, last modified on Nov 21, 2016

Changes to the 2017 network tariffs include:

  • the introduction of the opt-in residential and small cost-reflective demand tariffs; and
  • the introduction of a medium business tariff; and
  • reassigning small business customers with annual consumption greater than 60 megawatt

REASSIGNING Small Business Customers

We propose to reassign small business customers with an annual consumption greater than or equal to 60MWh and demand less than 120 kW to the new medium business cost-reflective tariff from 1 January 2017.

This tariff will transition to a fully cost-reflective tariff by 2019 following the approach outlined CitiPower’s revised TSS.
