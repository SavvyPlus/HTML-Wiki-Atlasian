Feed-In Tariff Scheme

These tariffs have been set to be used in customers with micro embedded generators (ie. solar panels). It requires the distributor to reimburse electricity retailers for the difference between the transitional and standard feed-in tariff rates. It applies to the energy 'exported' by commercial installations and 'imported' into the distribution system, where the customer is eligible for the transitional feed-in tariff rate.
