Created by Kate Daley, last modified on Jun 24, 2016

NEW Obsolete Tariffs

The Agreed Demand tariff (VLVS) will be closed to new small business applicants from 1 July 2016. This option is rarely a cost-effective or simple option for new small business customers so we will limit the tariff’s use to those currently using it:

Tariff Code
Name
Eligibility
Structure
VLVSLow Voltage Agreed Demand (KVA)A Low Voltage (kVA) Demand customer is a Distribution Network User generally taking supply at less than 1 kV and generally from the low voltage distribution transformer terminalsThis tariff applies a supply charge ($/day) and a usage rate for energy consumption ($/kWh). Demand is charged on a two block usage rate for energy consumption ($/kVA/mth). Block 1 is 1000kVA and Block2 is the balance usage. There is also an Additional Demand charge.
