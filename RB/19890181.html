7000Residential Demand

Offered from 1 July-2016. This new demand tariff is available to residential customers regardless of their size and cannot be used in conjunction with tariff 8400 or 8900. Customers must have a Type 1-4 meter to access this tariff.

This tariff has a daily supply charge ($/day), a Monthly Maximum Demand Charge ($/kW/mth) and a flat energy charge (c/kWh)
7300Smart Control (secondary Load tariff)Offered from 1 July 2016. Only available to residential customers on tariffs 7000, 8100 and 8300. This tariff has been developed with particular focus on emerging technologies such as batteries and electric vehicles.This tariff has a flat charge for all energy (c/kWh).

 

Tariff 7000 Residential Demand:

Code: DEMAND_ENERGEX_RESI

Maximum kilowatt demand measured as a single peak over a 30 minute period during peak charging window (between 4pm and 8pm on workdays during billing period). 

Workdays are weekdays but exclude government public holidays.
